Built for Woolworths Group Operations

One connected system for a $69B multi-banner retail group in transformation

As Woolworths Group consolidates its leadership structure, rebuilds its NSW supply chain network, and pushes AI deeper into stores and eCommerce, the teams driving that change are still running critical workflows across spreadsheets, PDFs, and disconnected tools. Airtable gives Operations one connected layer to plan, track, and scale execution — across Australian Food, BIG W, Cartology, and every service business in between.

Why Now

Dec 4, 2025 — Moorebank Site Visit

Woolworths Group confirmed Moorebank as the largest single capital investment in its history ($1.3B including leases), consolidating its NSW ambient network under a new Regional Distribution Centre — a major operating-model shift for supply chain teams to coordinate.

Feb 2026 — H1 FY26 Results

CEO Amanda Bardwell reported Group EBIT up 14.4% and highlighted a new DoorDash On Demand partnership, an Adelaide CFC opening, and an expanded Google partnership evolving "Olive" into an agentic AI shopping assistant — all requiring cross-functional operational coordination.

May 2026 — Q3 FY26 Trading Update

Group sales rose 4.5% to $18.1B with eCommerce up 23.8%, even as Bardwell flagged new cost and supply pressures from geopolitical disruption — underscoring the need for faster, more resilient planning across the Group's operating model.
