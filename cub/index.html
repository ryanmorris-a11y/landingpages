Why now

Four moves in 2026 that all run on connected information

Each of these is a program with many moving parts across sites, partners, and teams — exactly the kind of work that fragments across spreadsheets and stalls without a single source of truth.

Jun 2026

$150M Redbank distribution centre

Second of three new east-coast DCs, using a high-speed shuttle system and robotics, targeting a 5-Star Green Star rating and operational by 2028.

Airtable angle: one live view to coordinate fit-out, vendors, automation commissioning and go-live milestones across all three sites.
Source: Asahi Beverages, 9 Jun 2026
Mar 2026

The OneAsahi operating model

Described as the biggest change since the CUB acquisition in 2020 — a channel-focused model with dedicated reps and streamlined warehousing and distribution.

Airtable angle: unify the processes and hand-offs a channel-focused model depends on, so every team works from the same plan.
Source: National Liquor News, 11 Mar 2026
May 2026

Cool Soil barley-emissions program

Per-paddock emissions reports across nine Wimmera growers supplying Carlton Draught and Victoria Bitter, in partnership with Charles Sturt University and Riordan.

Airtable angle: turn fragmented, grower-level ESG data into one auditable, shareable source of truth for reporting and market access.
Source: The Shout, 1 May 2026
Feb 2026

Electric delivery fleet in Perth

Battery-electric trucks with Toll (ARENA co-funded) cut ~140 tonnes of emissions a year across 36,000+ deliveries — part of a broader decarbonisation push.

Airtable angle: track rollout, emissions data and partner deliverables in one connected system instead of scattered trackers.
Source: Asahi Beverages, 11 Feb 2026
