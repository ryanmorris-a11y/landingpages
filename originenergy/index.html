Why now

The priorities are set. The data can't wait for the re-platform.

Origin Energy is leading Australia's energy transition, targeting 4-5 GW of renewables and storage by 2030 and net-zero Scope 1/2/3 emissions by 2050, anchored by a $1.7B+ large-scale battery build-out (Eraring, Mortlake, Supernode) and the ~1.5 GW Yanco Delta Wind Farm progressing toward FID. It is scaling its Virtual Power Plant (now ~1.5 GW across 398,000+ connected services, using AI to orchestrate distributed assets) and growing its data centre energy customer segment via grid connections and long-term renewable contracts. The company is also progressing the formal separation of Octopus Energy and Kraken Technologies into standalone growth businesses, targeted for mid-2026, while maintaining disciplined cost-to-serve reductions ($100-150M target by FY26 vs FY24) and extending Eraring Power Station operations to April 2029 for grid reliability.
